# Neurophysiology

How the nervous system functions at the cellular and molecular level.

## Core Concepts

### Neural Communication

- [Neurotransmission](Neurotransmission.md) - How neurons communicate
- [Action Potential](Action%20potential.md) - The electrical signal in neurons
- [Neurotransmitters](Neurotransmitters.md) - Chemical messengers in the brain
- [Neuropeptides](Neuropeptides.md) - Peptide-based signaling molecules

### Neurotransmitter Effects

- [Agonists](Agonists.md) - Substances that enhance neurotransmitter activity
- [Antagonists](Antagonists.md) - Substances that block neurotransmitter activity

### Brain Adaptability

- [Plasticity](Plasticity.md) - The brain's ability to change and adapt

### Psychopharmacology

- [Psychoactive Drugs](Psychoactive%20drugs.md) - Substances that affect the brain

## Major Neurotransmitters

### Primary Neurotransmitters

- [Acetylcholine](List%20of%20Neurotransmitters/Acetylcholine.md) - Involved in learning and memory
- [GABA](List%20of%20Neurotransmitters/GABA.md) - The main inhibitory neurotransmitter
- [Glutamate](List%20of%20Neurotransmitters/Glutamate.md) - The main excitatory neurotransmitter
- [Anandamide](List%20of%20Neurotransmitters/Anandamide.md) - The endogenous cannabinoid

### Monoamines

- [Dopamine](List%20of%20Neurotransmitters/Monoamines/Dopamine.md) - Reward and motivation
- [Serotonin](List%20of%20Neurotransmitters/Monoamines/Serotonin.md) - Mood regulation
- [Norepinephrine (noradrenaline)](<List%20of%20Neurotransmitters/Monoamines/Norepinephrine%20(noradrenaline).md>) - Alertness and arousal
- [Epinephrine (adrenaline)](<List%20of%20Neurotransmitters/Monoamines/Epinephrine%20(adrenaline).md>) - Stress response
